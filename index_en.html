Bar Loungen

Susanne's Stugor Böda Hamn is a charming bar lounge nestled in a serene coastal setting. Here's a concise description for your bar lounge:

  • Delicious Bites: Indulge in a menu of flavorful small bites and bar snacks that perfectly complement your drinks.
  • Scenic Terrace: Enjoy breathtaking sea views from our outdoor terrace as you sip your favorite beverage.
  • Relaxed Atmosphere: Whether you seek a quiet spot or lively socializing, our bar lounge offers a welcoming and versatile space.
  • Friendly Service: Our attentive staff is dedicated to providing excellent service, ensuring your comfort and satisfaction.
  • Escape and Unwind: Susanne's Stugor Böda Hamn bar lounge is your retreat for leisure, socializing, and unforgettable moments.

Immerse yourself in the coastal charm of Susanne's Stugor Böda Hamn bar lounge—a place where relaxation, delightful drinks, and scenic views harmonize.

Cabins

Book Your Stay With Us

Welcome to Susanne's stugor Böda hamn, a charming destination offering cozy accommodations and a range of exciting activities. Here's all you need to know:

  • Accommodations: Choose from cabins with 1 or 2 bedrooms (subject to availability).
  • Sleeping Capacity: Accommodations can comfortably sleep 6 or 7 guests.
  • Activities and Services: Enjoy hiking, a children's playground, fishing, canoeing, and beachfront access.
  • Beach Proximity: The nearest beach is just 150 meters away.
  • Location: 1.8 km from the center of Böda.

We look forward to welcoming you to Susanne's stugor Böda hamn, where you can unwind, explore, and create wonderful memories amidst the beauty of our surroundings.
